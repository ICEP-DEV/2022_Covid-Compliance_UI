Why the Covid compliance system ?

Covid compliance system is the system that will better the covid 19 entrance screening at TUT that will be aligned with the regulations of covid-19n and help with keeping track or records of tests.

The purpose of this system is to assist in the adherence of covid-19 regulations when people enter out campuses. The regulations that the system will help with them being adhered to are social distancing, better ways of doing daily health checks, keeping records online of everyone that enters TUT campus and avoiding the touching of staff, student and visitor cards by officers and then giving them back to the owners.

 How to get started using the system?

First thing a User have to register on the system in order to be on our database before entering the campus and then afterwards login.

 How do I submit my health information to the system?

After a user has logged in, the health form will pop up which will need to be filled to notify the system of who and when the user has been at campus.

 Who is going check my health information and how will they check?

QR code will be sent to a user after submitting a health form, a user will scan that QR on the gate so an officer will be able to view a user profile on his site.
